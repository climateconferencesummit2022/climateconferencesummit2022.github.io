Long-Term

More

The long-term features of the database house all of your patients’ medical records including their medical history, previous prescriptions, etc. Our feature allows your practice to easily and quickly update this data. We also allow patients to update certain aspects of their history when they need to (your practice can determine which aspects of their medical record the patients can update).

Short-Term

More

The short-term aspects of this feature allow patients to directly upload/send data about their current illness/injury/problem to the doctor. In other words, the short-term part of the database will hold the patient’s data for their soonest appointment. This allows the patient to send pictures of their injury/illness, their temperature reading, etc., making it easier for your doctors to effectively help them. After the appointment, you can easily transfer the necessary information to the long-term portion of the database
